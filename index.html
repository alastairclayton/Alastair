International Recruitment Specialists for the Healthcare sector

Alastair Clayton is a London-based agency that specialises in recruiting personnel from overseas to work in the UK healthcare sector. We have two services under one roof. One is in helping healthcare companies obtain a sponsor licence that enables them to sponsor migrants under the Skilled Worker route, and the other is in helping the companies to source international talent. Our services also include helping the migrants come to the UK as a family, and not just on their own.

Assisting employers and employees alike in reaching their goals and achieving satisfaction is our aim, and with our personal experience and expertise in the healthcare sector and UK immigration law we are able to offer a full service down to the finest detail.

About Us

Demand for healthcare workers in the UK since Brexit has become very high and there are severe shortages, with care providers struggling to find permanent carers across a large variety of specialist categories. UK companies are now turning to international recruitment, and this is where our background and expertise can help companies recruit staff from overseas to reach their optimal level of service excellence.

Alastair Clayton’s business partnership includes intensive experience in international recruitment and immigration, in UK immigration law, and in dealing with complex immigration cases. We are experienced in the healthcare industry, but not limited to it, as we can also assist employers in any industry to obtain the sponsor licence and to employ migrants.

We offer practical solutions for our clients, with a complete range of services under one roof, from applying for a sponsor licence for employers in the UK to finding them the right talent in overseas locations.

Our Services

Employers/Service Providers

Skilled healthcare workers come under a diverse number of categories, including senior carers, engineers, IT technicians, physiotherapists, and other specialised occupations. Not all healthcare job roles can be sponsored, and the skill requirements are codified and listed under UK government immigration law.

An A-rated sponsor licence granted by the Home Office is a mandatory requirement for any employer wishing to employ a migrant worker in the UK. Many potential employers feel that this is a highly complex and expensive process, and that success is not necessarily guaranteed. Consequently, they are reluctant to begin.

International Recruitment

Our experience and our track record demonstrate that the process, when followed correctly, does not need to be difficult. We take all the burden of paperwork away from our clients, we operate to fixed fees, and our rates are competitive.

As we have been working in international recruitment for five years we have a significant presence among the Skilled Worker Visa seekers community, and the eyes to spot the right talent that will fit a company’s values.

The talents we source are mostly outside the UK, in their home country, or they are in the UK now pursuing higher education. We also partner with the Skilled Worker job seekers community platform.

Candidates

We accept CVs from international candidates who would like to pursue a career through a sponsorship in the UK, and we are able to provide CV writing, interview preparation, and other relevant training to help them.

Salaries vary for each occupation and job code based on the immigration rules. International candidates need to know that they enjoy the same employment rights as the UK workers and competitive wages, and that of course free healthcare is available in the UK.
